Flag-along!

Welcome to Flag-along!

Can you guess the country of the flag?

  • The game has three levels: EASY, MEDIUM and HARD.
  • Each level got 10 questions with four possible answers. Ang you got 60 seconds to guess all the flags per level!
  • You need a score of 700 to go to the medium level and at least 1500 to qualify for the hard level.
  • Can you make it to the highscore list of the top three players?
