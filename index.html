Welcome to FLAG-IT!

A game where you guess the country of the flag!

  • There are three levels of the game: EASY, MEDIUM and HARD.
  • Each level got 10 questions each with four possible answers. But you only got 60 seconds to guess all the flags per level!
  • You need a score of 700 to go to the medium level and at least 1500 to qualify for the hard level.
  • Can you make it to the highscore list of the top three players?
