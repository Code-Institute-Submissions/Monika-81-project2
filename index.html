Rules of the game:

  • Guess the country of the flag!
  • There are three levels of the game: EASY, MEDIUM and HARD.
  • Each level got 10 questions and each flag has four possible answers. But you only got 10 sekonds to guess the answer!
  • If you get 70% of the answers correct you can move to the next level (or you can start over).
  • At the end of the game you get your name at the highscore list if you are one of the top three players!
